<p><p>The COVID-19 detection kits market primarily includes two types: nucleic acid detection kits and antibody detection kits. Nucleic acid detection kits, such as PCR tests, identify the virus’s genetic material, allowing for accurate diagnosis during active infections. In contrast, antibody detection kits measure the presence of antibodies in the blood, indicating prior exposure to the virus. Both types play crucial roles in tracking the spread of COVID-19, guiding public health responses, and informing vaccination strategies.</p></p>
