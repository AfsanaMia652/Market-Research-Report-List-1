<p><ul><li>Herbicide-tolerance (HT)</li><li>Insect-resistance (IR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional seeds market is characterized by various types, notably herbicide-tolerant (HT) and insect-resistant (IR) seeds. HT seeds allow farmers to apply herbicides without harming the crop, facilitating weed control and boosting yields. In contrast, IR seeds are genetically modified to resist specific pests, reducing the need for chemical insecticides and lowering crop losses. Both types enhance agricultural productivity and sustainability, appealing to farmers seeking efficient and cost-effective solutions for their crop management challenges.</p></p>
