<p><strong>The Cation Exchange Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cation exchange resins are categorized primarily into Type I and Type II, based on their chemistry and application. Type I resins are strong acid cation exchange resins, featuring sulfonic acid groups, suitable for a wide pH range and effective in high ion concentration environments. Type II resins, on the other hand, are weak acid cation exchange resins with carboxylic acid groups, ideal for lower pH applications and specialized for selective ion removal and softening processes in water treatment.</p></p>
