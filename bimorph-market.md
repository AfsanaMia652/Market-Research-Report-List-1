<p><strong>The Bimorph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Bimorph</li><li>Bimetal Bimorph</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bimorph market can be categorized into two main types: piezoelectric bimorphs and bimetal bimorphs. Piezoelectric bimorphs utilize materials that generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for precision applications like actuators and sensors. In contrast, bimetal bimorphs consist of two metals with different thermal expansion coefficients, causing bending in response to temperature changes, which is commonly used in thermostats and switches. Both types serve specialized functions in various industries, enhancing efficiency and functionality.</p></p>
