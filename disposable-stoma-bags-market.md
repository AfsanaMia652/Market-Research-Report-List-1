<p><p>The disposable stoma bags market comprises two main types: one-piece and two-piece bags. One-piece bags combine the pouch and skin barrier into a single unit, offering simplicity and ease of use, making them ideal for users seeking convenience. In contrast, two-piece bags separate the pouch from the skin barrier, allowing for easier pouch changes and customization, which appeals to those prioritizing comfort and flexibility. Both types cater to different user preferences and offer solutions for effective stoma care.</p></p>
