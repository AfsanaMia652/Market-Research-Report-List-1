<p><p>The colored PU foams market encompasses two primary types: rigid and flexible PU foams. Rigid PU foams are characterized by their hard structure, providing excellent thermal insulation and structural support, making them ideal for applications like construction and refrigeration. In contrast, flexible PU foams are soft and versatile, commonly used in mattresses, upholstery, and automotive seating. Both types can be produced in various colors to enhance aesthetics and meet diverse industry requirements, catering to consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
