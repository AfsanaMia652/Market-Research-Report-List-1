<p><p>The water-soluble compound fertilizer market is segmented into liquid and solid fertilizers. Liquid fertilizers are typically mixed with water for easy application, ensuring rapid nutrient absorption by plants. They often facilitate precision feeding and are preferred for foliar application. Solid fertilizers, on the other hand, are available in granular or powdered forms and dissolve in water to release nutrients. They are commonly used for soil application, providing a slow-release nutrient supply and improving soil health over time.</p></p>
