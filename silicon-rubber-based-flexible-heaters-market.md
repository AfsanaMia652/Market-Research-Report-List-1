<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound Heaters</li><li>Etched Foil Heaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicon rubber-based flexible heaters are categorized into two main types: wire-wound heaters and etched foil heaters. Wire-wound heaters use a heating element made of wire wrapped around a core, providing uniform heat distribution and flexibility for applications requiring high durability. In contrast, etched foil heaters feature a thin metallic foil pattern etched onto a flexible substrate, offering precise heat distribution and quicker heating response. Both types are widely used in industries like automotive, medical, and aerospace for their versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
