<p><p>The VPN services market primarily consists of two main types: Remote Access VPN and Site-to-Site VPN. Remote Access VPN allows individual users to securely connect to a private network over the internet, often used by remote workers to access corporate resources. In contrast, Site-to-Site VPN connects entire networks, enabling secure communication between different office locations. This type is commonly employed by enterprises to ensure secure data exchange and collaboration across geographically dispersed sites, enhancing overall network security.</p></p>
