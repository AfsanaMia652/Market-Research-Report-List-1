<p><p>The enzymatic debridement market is segmented into three types: collagenase products, papain products, and others. Collagenase products are derived from bacteria and effectively break down collagen in wounds, promoting healing. Papain products, derived from papaya, help remove necrotic tissue and stimulate tissue regeneration. The "others" category encompasses various alternative enzymatic agents used in wound care. Each type offers unique benefits for managing wound healing and debridement, catering to different patient needs and treatment preferences.</p></p>
