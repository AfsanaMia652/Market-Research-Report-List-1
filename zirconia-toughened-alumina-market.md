<p><strong>The Zirconia Toughened Alumina Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fibre Toughening</li><li>Particle Toughening</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Zirconia Toughened Alumina (ZTA) is enhanced using two primary toughening methods: fiber toughening and particle toughening. Fiber toughening involves incorporating continuous fibers into the alumina matrix, improving toughness and resistance to crack propagation. Particle toughening, on the other hand, consists of dispersing zirconia particles within the alumina, which enhance mechanical properties through a toughening mechanism that mitigates crack growth. Both methods aim to improve the durability and performance of ZTA, making it suitable for demanding applications in various industries.</p></p>
