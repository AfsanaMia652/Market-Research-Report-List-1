<p><strong>The Cooking Thermometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instant-Read</li><li>In-Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooking thermometer market consists of various types, primarily instant-read and in-probe thermometers. Instant-read thermometers provide quick temperature measurements by inserting the probe into food for a few seconds, making them ideal for home cooks seeking efficiency. In-probe thermometers, on the other hand, are designed for longer-term monitoring, often featuring a probe that remains in the food while it cooks, allowing for continuous temperature reading. Both types enhance cooking accuracy, leading to better food safety and quality.</p></p>
