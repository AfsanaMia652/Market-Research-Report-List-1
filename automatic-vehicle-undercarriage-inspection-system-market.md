<p><p>The Automatic Vehicle Undercarriage Inspection System (AVUIS) market includes two main types: fixed and mobile systems. Fixed systems are permanently installed at inspection sites, providing continuous, high-traffic capability for thorough evaluations of vehicles' undercarriages. In contrast, mobile systems are portable, allowing inspections to occur at various locations, making them versatile for use in dynamic environments such as roadside checks or temporary events. Both types enhance safety by facilitating quick and efficient inspections to identify potential issues that may arise in vehicle undercarriages.</p></p>
