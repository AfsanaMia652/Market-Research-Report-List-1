<p><strong>The COVID 19 IgMIgG Rapid Test Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RDT(Rapid Diagnostic Test)</li><li>ELISA(Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Aassay)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 IgM/IgG rapid test kits market consists of three main types: RDTs, ELISA, and Neutralization Assays. RDTs provide quick, qualitative results for antibody detection, making them suitable for point-of-care testing. ELISA offers higher sensitivity and specificity, quantifying antibodies, and is often used in laboratory settings. Neutralization Assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to prevent virus entry into cells, serving mainly in research and vaccine development, providing insights into immune response effectiveness.</p></p>
