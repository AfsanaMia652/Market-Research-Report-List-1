<p><p>The medical ultrasound knife market is categorized into two primary types: suction type and cutting type. Suction type ultrasound knives utilize ultrasonic technology to emulsify tissue while simultaneously suctioning away debris, thereby minimizing collateral damage and improving visibility during surgery. In contrast, cutting type ultrasound knives focus on precise tissue cutting through high-frequency vibrations, enhancing surgical accuracy. Both types are instrumental in minimally invasive procedures, offering benefits such as reduced recovery times and improved patient outcomes in various medical specialties.</p></p>
