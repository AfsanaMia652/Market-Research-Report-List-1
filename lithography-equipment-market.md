<p><strong>The Lithography Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mask Aligner</li><li>Laser Direct Imaging</li><li>Projection</li><li>Laser Ablation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithography equipment market encompasses various technologies for transferring patterns onto substrates. Mask aligners utilize photomasks to project images onto the wafer, suitable for low-volume production. Laser direct imaging eliminates the need for masks, using lasers to directly write patterns, enhancing flexibility. Projection lithography relies on optical projection systems for high-resolution imaging, ideal for advanced semiconductor manufacturing. Laser ablation selectively removes material to create patterns, offering precision and versatility in various applications, including microfabrication and circuit design.</p></p>
