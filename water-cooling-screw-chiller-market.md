<p><p>The water cooling screw chiller market is categorized into two main types based on adjustment mechanisms: electrodeless adjustment and sectional adjustment. </p><p>Electrodeless adjustment allows for continuous modulation of cooling capacity, enhancing efficiency and energy savings by adapting to varying load conditions. In contrast, sectional adjustment involves fixed capacities, where the chiller operates at predetermined levels, requiring manual intervention for changes. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, balancing performance, efficiency, and operational flexibility in cooling systems.</p></p>
