<p><p>Change control management software is categorized into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and ease of access from anywhere with an internet connection. This model often includes automatic updates and lower upfront costs. In contrast, on-premise software is installed directly on a company’s hardware, providing greater control over data and security. However, it typically requires higher initial investment and ongoing maintenance, making it suitable for organizations with specific compliance requirements.</p></p>
