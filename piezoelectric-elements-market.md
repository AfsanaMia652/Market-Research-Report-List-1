<p><p>The piezoelectric elements market is primarily divided into three types: PZT-based, PMN-based, and others. PZT-based elements, made from lead zirconate titanate, dominate due to their high efficiency and versatility in applications like sensors and actuators. PMN-based elements, composed of lead magnesium niobate, offer superior performance in high-frequency devices but are often more expensive. The "others" category encompasses various materials, including polymers and ceramics, catering to niche applications requiring specific performance characteristics. Each type serves distinct industries, enhancing technological solutions.</p></p>
