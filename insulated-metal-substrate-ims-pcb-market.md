<p><p>The Insulated Metal Substrate (IMS) PCB market features three primary types based on core materials: aluminum, stainless steel, and copper cores. Aluminum core IMS PCBs are the most popular due to their lightweight, excellent thermal conductivity, and cost-effectiveness, making them ideal for LED applications. Stainless steel cores offer higher mechanical strength and corrosion resistance, suitable for harsh environments. In contrast, copper core IMS PCBs provide superior thermal and electrical conductivity, benefiting high-performance applications requiring efficient heat dissipation.</p></p>
