<p><ul><li>Rectangular Waveguide Switch</li><li>Double Ridge Waveguide Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric waveguide switches market includes two primary types: rectangular waveguide switches and double ridge waveguide switches. Rectangular waveguide switches are known for their simplicity and efficiency in controlling microwave signals within a rectangular cross-section, making them widely used in various applications. Double ridge waveguide switches, on the other hand, feature a wider cross-section, enhancing bandwidth and reducing losses, thus supporting higher frequency ranges. Both types are crucial in telecommunications, radar, and satellite systems for signal routing and management.</p></p>
