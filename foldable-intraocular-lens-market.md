<p><p>Foldable intraocular lenses (IOLs) are prosthetic devices implanted in the eye to replace the natural lens following cataract surgery or to correct refractive errors. Unlike traditional rigid lenses, foldable IOLs can be compressed for insertion through smaller incisions, promoting quicker recovery and minimizing surgical trauma. This innovative design has positioned them as a preferred choice in ophthalmic procedures.</p><p>The Foldable Intraocular Lens Market is experiencing significant growth, anticipated to expand at a CAGR of 11.40% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the rising prevalence of cataracts, an aging population, and increased awareness of advanced surgical options. Moreover, technological advancements in lens materials and designs, including the development of multifocal and toric options, are enhancing patient outcomes and satisfaction.</p><p>Current market trends indicate a shift towards premium IOLs, reflecting consumer demand for improved vision quality and convenience. Additionally, the integration of telemedicine in pre-operative assessments and post-operative care is revolutionizing patient management in the lens implantation process.
