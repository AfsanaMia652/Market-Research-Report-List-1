<p><p>The drying baker market comprises four main types based on temperature: Low-temperature drying bakers, which operate at temperatures below 100°C, are ideal for delicate foods to preserve flavor and nutrients. Room temperature drying bakers use ambient conditions for gentle drying, suitable for items requiring minimal heat impact. High-temperature drying bakers operate above 100°C, maximizing efficiency for broader applications. Ultra-high temperature drying bakers exceed 150°C, enabling rapid drying processes, often used in commercial food production for enhanced shelf-life and safety.</p></p>
