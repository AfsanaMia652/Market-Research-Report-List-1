<p><p>The Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) device market includes various types designed to diagnose and treat the condition. Esophagoscopy and gastroscopy involve visual examinations of the esophagus and stomach. Fundoplication devices aid in surgical interventions by preventing acid reflux. Manometry systems assess esophageal function, while pH monitoring systems measure acid levels. Radio frequency ablation devices utilize heat to destroy abnormal tissue, and sphincter augmentation devices enhance esophageal barrier function. Ultrasonic surgical endostaplers are used for precise tissue sealing during procedures.</p></p>
