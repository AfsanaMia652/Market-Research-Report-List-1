<p><p>The Micro-Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) market comprises several types based on design. Open MRI features a wide, open design, enhancing patient comfort but often offering lower resolution. Closed MRI provides a traditional, enclosed experience with high image quality but may cause discomfort for some patients. Standard Bore MRI has a narrow opening, balancing comfort and imaging capability. Wide-Bore MRI combines a spacious opening with high-resolution imaging, catering to larger patients and those with claustrophobia while maintaining diagnostic efficacy.</p></p>
