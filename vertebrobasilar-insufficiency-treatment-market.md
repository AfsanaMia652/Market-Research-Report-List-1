<p><p>Vertebrobasilar insufficiency treatment market encompasses various drug types. Antihistamines are used to alleviate dizziness and vertigo by stabilizing inner ear balance. Anticholinergic agents help reduce the frequency of dizziness episodes by blocking specific neurotransmitters. Antiplatelet agents prevent blood clots, improving blood flow to the brain's posterior circulation. Vasodilators work by expanding blood vessels, enhancing circulation. Together, these medications aim to alleviate symptoms and enhance blood flow, addressing the challenges posed by vertebrobasilar insufficiency effectively.</p></p>
