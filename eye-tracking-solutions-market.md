<p><p>The eye tracking solutions market encompasses three main types: Eye Attached Tracking, Optical Tracking, and Electrooculography. Eye Attached Tracking involves wearable devices that directly attach to the user, offering high precision for applications like virtual and augmented reality. Optical Tracking utilizes cameras to monitor eye movements from a distance, ideal for research and user experience studies. Electrooculography measures electrical activity around the eyes using sensors, providing a non-intrusive method suitable for medical diagnostics and cognitive research.</p></p>
