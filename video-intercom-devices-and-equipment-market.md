<p><p>Video intercom devices are communication systems that enable visual and audio interaction between users, often used in buildings for security and convenience. The market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and IP types. Analog video intercoms use traditional wiring and provide basic video and audio quality, making them cost-effective for small setups. In contrast, IP video intercoms leverage internet protocols, offering advanced features like remote access, higher video quality, and integration with smart home systems, making them suitable for larger or more sophisticated installations.</p></p>
