<p><p>The bamboo fibers market is divided into two main types: natural bamboo fiber and chemical bamboo fiber. Natural bamboo fiber is derived from the raw bamboo plant through mechanical processes, retaining its ecological benefits and biodegradable properties, making it ideal for sustainable textiles. On the other hand, chemical bamboo fiber involves a more industrial process where chemicals are used to convert bamboo into fiber. This method often yields softer textiles but raises concerns about environmental impact due to chemical usage.</p></p>
