<p><p>Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB) is a critical safety feature in vehicles designed to prevent collisions by automatically applying the brakes when a potential crash is detected. The market consists of several types: Forward Collision Warning alerts drivers of impending collisions, Dynamic Brake Support enhances braking force during emergency situations without full intervention, and Crash Imminent Braking engages the brakes with full force to minimize impact when a collision is unavoidable. Together, these technologies aim to enhance road safety and reduce accident severity.</p></p>
