<p><p>The multipurpose label adhesive market comprises two primary types: water-based and solvent-based adhesives. Water-based label adhesives utilize water as the primary solvent, offering environmental benefits and safer application. They are ideal for paper labels and quick-drying applications. In contrast, solvent-based label adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong adhesion and durability, especially on challenging surfaces. These adhesives are commonly used for industrial and outdoor applications where resistance to moisture and temperatures is crucial. Both types cater to diverse labeling needs across various industries.</p></p>
