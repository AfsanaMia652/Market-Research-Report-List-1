<p><p>The anti-corrosion coating market is segmented into water-based, solvent-based, and other types. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and environmental benefits, making them ideal for residential applications. Solvent-based coatings have a higher concentration of active materials, providing robust protection in harsh environments but releasing more VOCs. Other coatings may include powder coatings and hybrid systems, which combine various technologies to enhance durability and performance against corrosion in diverse applications.</p></p>
