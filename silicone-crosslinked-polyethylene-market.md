<p><p>The Silicone Crosslinked Polyethylene (SiPE) market comprises three main types: Extrusion Grade, Injection Molding Grade, and Stripping Grade. Extrusion Grade is designed for continuous processes, producing parts with uniform cross-sections, ideal for tubing and profiles. Injection Molding Grade is utilized for creating complex shapes with high precision, suitable for intricate components. Stripping Grade serves specialized applications, allowing for easy removal of insulation in cables and wires, enhancing efficiency in assembly. Each type caters to distinct manufacturing needs and applications.</p></p>
