<p><p>The Battery Management Information Systems (BMIS) market encompasses two primary types: Centralized BMS and Distributed BMS. Centralized BMS relies on a single control unit to manage battery functions, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for small to medium applications. In contrast, Distributed BMS employs multiple control units, enabling better scalability and enhanced monitoring in larger systems like electric vehicles and renewable energy storage. This allows for improved efficiency, safety, and performance across various battery configurations.</p></p>
