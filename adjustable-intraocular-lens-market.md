<p><strong>The Adjustable Intraocular Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Foldable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Adjustable Intraocular Lens market is primarily divided into two types: rigid and foldable lenses. Rigid lenses are made from materials that maintain their shape, providing stable vision correction but may require larger incisions during surgery. In contrast, foldable lenses are designed to be compact, allowing them to be inserted through smaller incisions, which can reduce recovery time and complications. Both types serve the purpose of improving vision post-cataract surgery, catering to different surgical preferences and patient needs.</p></p>
