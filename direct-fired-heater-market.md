<p><p>The Direct Fired Heater market includes three primary types: Radiant Heaters, Convection Heaters, and Radiant-Convection Heaters. Radiant Heaters emit infrared radiation to directly heat objects and surfaces, making them efficient for spot heating. Convection Heaters warm the air around them, providing uniform space heating ideal for larger areas. Radiant-Convection Heaters combine both methods, delivering quick warmth and improved air circulation. Each type serves different applications, highlighting their adaptability in various industrial and commercial environments.</p></p>
