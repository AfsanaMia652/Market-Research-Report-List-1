<p><p>The Creatine Kinase Test Kit market comprises two primary types: the Enzyme Coupling Method and the Colorimetry Method. The Enzyme Coupling Method utilizes enzymatic reactions to amplify the detection of creatine kinase, providing high sensitivity and specificity. In contrast, the Colorimetry Method relies on color change in a reaction to quantify creatine kinase levels, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Both methods serve critical roles in diagnosing conditions like myocardial infarction and muscle disorders, catering to diverse laboratory needs.</p></p>
