<p><p>Patient Lift Pendants (PLPs) are devices used to assist caregivers in lifting and transferring patients safely. The market is divided into two main types: Single-Mast and Twin-Mast systems. Single-Mast PLPs are typically more compact and suitable for smaller spaces, providing essential support for individual transfers. In contrast, Twin-Mast PLPs offer enhanced stability and load capacity, making them ideal for larger or heavier patients, and are often used in more spacious healthcare facilities for increased efficiency and safety.</p></p>
