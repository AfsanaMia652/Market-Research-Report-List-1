<p><p>Smart electric bidet seats are categorized into three main types based on their water heating mechanisms. Tank type bidets store and heat a specific amount of water in a tank, providing a steady supply for each use. Tank-less types heat water on demand, ensuring an endless supply but requiring a higher power draw. Hybrid models combine a small tank for quick heating with on-demand heating for continuous use, offering the best of both worlds in efficiency and performance.</p></p>
