<p><p>Polyurethane sealants are essential in construction for their flexibility and durability. The market is divided into two main types: single component and multi-component. Single-component sealants are ready-to-use and cure through exposure to moisture in the air, making them convenient for quick applications. In contrast, multi-component sealants require mixing before usage, allowing for tailored properties and enhanced performance, such as improved adhesion or chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in construction for sealing joints, gaps, and providing waterproofing solutions.</p></p>
