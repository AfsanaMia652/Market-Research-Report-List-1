<p><p>Polymer blends and alloys are mixtures of different polymers designed to enhance properties for specific applications. PC-based blends and alloys utilize polycarbonate for improved strength and heat resistance, making them suitable for automotive and electronic components. On the other hand, PPO/PPE-based blends leverage polyphenylene oxide and polyphenylene ether to deliver excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, commonly used in electrical and industrial applications. Both types serve niche needs by combining the advantageous features of their constituent polymers.</p></p>
