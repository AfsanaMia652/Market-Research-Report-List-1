<p><ul><li>XPAPR</li><li>PE-X</li><li>PAP</li><li>PP-B</li><li>PP-R</li><li>PB</li><li>PE-RT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor heating tube market comprises various types, each suited for specific applications. XPAP is known for its excellent oxygen barrier and resistance to corrosion. PE-X offers flexibility and durability, making it popular in radiant heating systems. PAP combines aluminum and polyethylene for better thermal efficiency. PP-B and PP-R are polypropylene materials that provide good thermal properties and resistance to chemicals. Lastly, PB, or polybutylene, is flexible and suited for low-temperature applications, enhancing comfort in floor heating systems.</p></p>
