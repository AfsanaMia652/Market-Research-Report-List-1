<p><strong>The Automotive Optical Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Lens</li><li>Aspherical Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive optical lens market primarily features two types: spherical lenses and aspherical lenses. Spherical lenses, with a consistent curvature, are commonly used in headlights and taillights due to their straightforward manufacturing process and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, aspherical lenses boast a more complex shape, which allows for improved light distribution, reduced aberrations, and enhanced aesthetics. Aspherical lenses are increasingly preferred in advanced lighting systems, contributing to better visibility and safety while also meeting modern design trends in automotive applications.</p></p>
