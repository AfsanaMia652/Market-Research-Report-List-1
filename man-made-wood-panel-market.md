<p><p>The man-made wood panel market comprises several key types, each serving distinct applications. Particle board is made from wood chips and adhesives, commonly used in furniture. Plywood consists of thin layers of veneer glued together for strength and versatility. Oriented strand board (OSB) is made of strands arranged in layers, ideal for structural uses. Medium-density fiberboard (MDF) and high-density fiberboard (HDF) offer smooth surfaces for cabinetry and moldings. Hardboard, denser than MDF, is used in hard surfaces. Other categories may include laminate and specialty panels.</p></p>
