<p><p>Restaurant catering systems can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premises markets. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet, providing flexibility, scalability, and ease of updates. They enable real-time collaboration and access from multiple locations. On-premises systems, in contrast, are installed locally on the restaurant’s hardware, offering greater control over data security and performance. While they typically require higher upfront costs, they can provide more tailored solutions for specific operational needs within the restaurant.</p></p>
