<p><p>Biopsy guidance systems are essential tools in accurately obtaining tissue samples for diagnosis. Stereotactic guided biopsy uses precise, 3D imaging to target lesions, often in the breast or brain. Ultrasound guided biopsy employs real-time ultrasound imaging to visualize tissues, commonly used for soft tissue or abdominal lesions. MRI guided biopsy relies on magnetic resonance imaging for high-contrast visualization, facilitating sampling of challenging locations, such as the prostate or liver. Each type enhances diagnostic accuracy and patient outcomes.</p></p>
