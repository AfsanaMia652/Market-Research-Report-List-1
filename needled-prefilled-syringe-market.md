<p><p>The needled prefilled syringe market is categorized primarily into glass and plastic types. Glass syringes offer excellent barrier properties and stability, making them suitable for sensitive biologics and vaccines. They are often preferred for their inertness and protection against leakage. In contrast, plastic syringes, typically made from polymers, are lightweight and shatter-resistant, enhancing safety during transportation and handling. The choice between glass and plastic often depends on factors such as drug compatibility, manufacturing costs, and end-user preferences.</p></p>
