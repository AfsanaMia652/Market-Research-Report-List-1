<p><p>The concrete bonding agents market comprises various types, primarily including cementitious latex-based and epoxy-based agents. Cementitious latex-based bonding agents enhance the adhesion between new and existing concrete through a polymer-modified composition, improving flexibility and resistance to cracking. In contrast, epoxy-based bonding agents offer superior strength and chemical resistance, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types serve to improve bond strength, durability, and overall performance in construction and repair processes, catering to different project requirements and conditions.</p></p>
