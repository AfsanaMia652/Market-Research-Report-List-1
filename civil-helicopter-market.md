<p><ul><li>Light Helicopter (Below 4.5 T)</li><li>Medium Helicopter (4.5 to 8.5 T)</li><li>Heavy Helicopter (Above 8.5 T)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The civil helicopter market is segmented into three categories based on weight. Light helicopters, weighing below 4.5 tons, are typically used for private, tourism, and medical purposes due to their agility and lower operating costs. Medium helicopters, ranging from 4.5 to 8.5 tons, serve various roles including emergency services, cargo transport, and corporate transportation. Heavy helicopters, exceeding 8.5 tons, are designed for demanding operations like offshore transport, search and rescue, and large-scale construction projects, offering greater capacity and power.</p></p>
