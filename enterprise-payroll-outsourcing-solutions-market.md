<p><p>Enterprise Payroll Outsourcing Solutions can be classified into two primary types: Full-Managed Outsourcing and Co-Managed Outsourcing. </p><p>Full-Managed Outsourcing involves a third-party provider taking complete responsibility for all payroll functions, including calculations, tax filings, and compliance, allowing businesses to focus on core activities. </p><p>In contrast, Co-Managed Outsourcing entails a partnership where the provider and the company's internal team collaboratively manage payroll tasks. This model allows organizations to maintain some control while benefiting from external expertise and technology for improved efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
