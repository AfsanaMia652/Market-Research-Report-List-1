<p><p>The deep penetrating primer market is categorized into one-component and two-component types. One-component primers are ready-to-use formulations that require no mixing; they provide ease of application and suitable adhesion for a variety of surfaces. In contrast, two-component primers consist of a base and a hardener that must be combined before use, resulting in superior durability and chemical resistance. Each type serves specific applications, catering to differing user needs in construction, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
