<p><p>Autonomous directional drilling encompasses various market types, primarily focusing on inclination directional wells and horizontal wells. Horizontal wells, on the other hand, extend horizontally within the reservoir, enhancing production capabilities by accessing a larger area of oil or gas. Both types utilize advanced technologies to improve precision, reduce operational costs, and minimize environmental impact in the drilling process.</p></p>
