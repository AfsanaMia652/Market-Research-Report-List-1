<p><p>The gold plating chemicals market is primarily divided into two types: cyanide gold plating chemicals and cyanide-free gold plating chemicals. Cyanide gold plating chemicals utilize cyanide compounds to enable effective metal deposition on substrates, providing high-quality finishes but posing environmental and health risks. In contrast, cyanide-free gold plating chemicals offer a safer alternative, employing non-toxic formulations that meet regulatory standards while still delivering reliable plating performance. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including electronics and jewelry manufacturing.</p></p>
