<p><p>AI Platform Cloud Services operate within three primary market types: Public Clouds, Private Clouds, and Hybrid Clouds. Public Clouds offer shared resources over the internet, providing scalability and cost-efficiency for businesses. Private Clouds, on the other hand, deliver dedicated resources for individual organizations, enhancing security and control over sensitive data. Hybrid Clouds combine both public and private infrastructures, allowing businesses to optimize resource allocation, improve flexibility, and manage workloads according to specific needs, balancing cost with security.</p></p>
