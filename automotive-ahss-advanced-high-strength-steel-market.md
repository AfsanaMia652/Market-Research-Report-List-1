<p><p>The Automotive AHSS (Advanced High-Strength Steel) market comprises several types, each tailored for specific performance characteristics. Dual Phase Steel offers a combination of strength and ductility, ideal for crash-resistant applications. Martensitic Steel provides high tensile strength suitable for structural components. Boron Steel is enhanced for hardening processes, making it vital for safety parts. TRIP Steel, with its unique transformation mechanisms, balances strength and formability, contributing to lightweight, energy-absorbing structures in vehicles, enhancing safety and efficiency.</p></p>
