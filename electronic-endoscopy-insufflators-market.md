<p><p>The Electronic Endoscopy Insufflators market is categorized into two main types: those without gas preheating functions and those with gas preheating functions. Insufflators without gas preheating deliver gas directly at room temperature, suitable for standard procedures. In contrast, insufflators with gas preheating features warm the gas before introduction, minimizing discomfort and improving visualization during endoscopy. The choice between these types depends on procedural requirements, patient comfort, and the specific capabilities of the endoscopic equipment used.</p></p>
