<p><p>The Automotive Driver Monitoring System (DMS) focuses on enhancing safety through real-time assessment of driver behavior and condition. It encompasses two main market types: Driver State Monitoring and Driver Health Monitoring. Driver State Monitoring evaluates cognitive states like drowsiness and distraction, employing cameras and sensors to track eye movement and facial expressions. In contrast, Driver Health Monitoring assesses physical health factors, such as heart rate and fatigue levels, ensuring that drivers remain fit to operate vehicles safely. Both contribute to improved road safety and driver awareness.</p></p>
