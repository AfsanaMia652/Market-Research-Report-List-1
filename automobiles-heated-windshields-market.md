<p><p>The automotive heated windshields market comprises two main types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields utilize thin filaments integrated within the glass to generate heat, effectively melting ice and snow for improved visibility. In contrast, heated coated windshields feature a transparent conductive coating that evenly distributes heat across the surface, enhancing clarity and defogging capabilities. Both technologies aim to enhance driver safety and comfort by ensuring clear visibility in cold or inclement weather conditions.</p></p>
