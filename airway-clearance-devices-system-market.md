<p><p>The Airway Clearance Devices System market comprises various types designed to enhance mucus clearance in patients with respiratory conditions. Positive Expiratory Pressure (PEP) devices create pressure during exhalation to open airways and facilitate mucus removal. Flutter devices utilize oscillatory pressure to help expel mucus from the lungs. Other devices may include high-frequency chest wall oscillation systems or handheld nebulizers. Each type serves to improve lung function and enhance patient quality of life by promoting effective airway clearance.</p></p>
