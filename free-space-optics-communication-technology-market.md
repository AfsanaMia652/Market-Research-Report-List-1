<p><p>Free Space Optics (FSO) communication technology involves several key market components. Transmitters convert electrical signals into light for transmission, while receivers capture and convert light back into electrical signals. Modulators control the light signal's characteristics to encode data, whereas demodulators retrieve the original information from the modulated signal. Other elements in this market may include lenses, filters, and alignment systems that enhance signal quality and reliability, facilitating point-to-point high-speed wireless communication over short to medium distances.</p></p>
