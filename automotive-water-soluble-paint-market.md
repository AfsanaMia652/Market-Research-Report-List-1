<p><p>The automotive water-soluble paint market comprises various resin types, each offering unique properties. Polyurethane resins offer superior gloss and abrasion resistance, making them ideal for automotive finishes. Epoxy resins deliver strong adhesion and chemical resistance, whereas alkyd resins are valued for their ease of application and finish quality.
