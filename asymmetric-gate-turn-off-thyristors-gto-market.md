<p><p>Asymmetric Gate Turn Off Thyristors (GTOs) are critical components in high-power electronics, available in two market types: Buffer Layer and Standard. Buffer Layer GTOs incorporate a protective layer that enhances thermal management and improves performance during rapid switching, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, Standard GTOs offer reliability and efficiency in less demanding environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial requirements, with Buffer Layer GTOs preferred for high-frequency operations and Standard GTOs used in conventional power control applications.</p></p>
