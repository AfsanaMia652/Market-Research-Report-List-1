<p><ul><li>Two-Phase Aerosol</li><li>Three-Phase Aerosol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Air Care Aerosol market is categorized into two primary types: Two-Phase Aerosol and Three-Phase Aerosol. Two-Phase Aerosols consist of both liquid and gaseous phases, utilizing a propellant to disperse the product effectively. They are commonly found in air fresheners and room sprays. In contrast, Three-Phase Aerosols have a distinct separation of liquid, gas, and a third phase, often containing small particles or additional substances, delivering longer-lasting fragrances and enhanced dispersion, typically used in more specialized air care applications.</p></p>
