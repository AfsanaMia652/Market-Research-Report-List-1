<p><ul><li>Array</li><li>Cell</li><li>Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The panel testing equipment market comprises three main types: array, cell, and module. Array testing focuses on evaluating the performance and efficiency of multiple solar cells interconnected as a single unit, ensuring optimal energy output. Cell testing involves assessing individual photovoltaic cells for parameters like efficiency and durability. Module testing evaluates complete solar panels, considering factors like weather resistance and overall energy generation capacity. Together, these testing types ensure the reliability and effectiveness of solar technologies in various applications.</p></p>
