<p><p>The automotive washer pumps market features primarily two types: mono pumps and dual pumps. Mono pumps consist of a single motor and impeller, powering a singular fluid flow, ideal for basic windshield washer systems. In contrast, dual pumps encompass two separate units, allowing for increased flow control and the ability to service multiple washing functions simultaneously, such as front and rear windshield washing. Both types cater to different vehicle needs, enhancing visibility and safety for drivers.</p></p>
