<p><p>The silica flour filler market includes various types such as crystal quartz powder, fused silica powder, and spherical quartz powder. Crystal quartz powder is derived from natural quartz and is commonly used in construction and glass industries due to its high purity and hardness. Fused silica powder, created by melting high-purity silica, offers superior thermal resistance and is utilized in ceramics and high-performance applications. Spherical quartz powder, known for its uniform shape, enhances flow properties and is preferred in coatings and composites.</p></p>
