<p><p>The Automotive Battery Thermal Management System market encompasses two primary types: liquid cooling and heating, and air cooling and heating. Liquid cooling systems utilize fluids to efficiently transfer heat away from the battery, maintaining optimal temperature levels and enhancing performance. In contrast, air cooling and heating systems rely on airflow to manage battery temperatures, offering a simpler and lighter solution. Both systems are crucial for maximizing battery life, efficiency, and safety in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
