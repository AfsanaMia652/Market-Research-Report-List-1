<p><p>The cordierite market primarily consists of two types: sintered cordierite and porous cordierite. Sintered cordierite is characterized by its high thermal stability and mechanical strength, making it ideal for applications like kiln furniture and substrates in catalytic converters. Porous cordierite, on the other hand, features a lightweight, porous structure, advantageous for thermal management in industrial processes and as a filtration medium. Both types cater to diverse industries, including ceramics, automotive, and electronics, driven by their unique physical properties.</p></p>
