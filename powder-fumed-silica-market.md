<p><strong>The Powder Fumed Silica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BET 100-160</li><li>BET 160-210</li><li>BET 210-300</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder fumed silica is categorized based on its surface area measured in BET (Brunauer-Emmett-Teller) method, which influences its properties and applications. Types include BET 100-160, suitable for applications requiring moderate surface area and low viscosity; BET 160-210, offering higher surface area for improved thickening and reinforcing; BET 210-300, for specialized applications needing maximum absorption and thickening; and Others, encompassing variations with unique characteristics for niche markets. Each type serves distinct industrial needs in coatings, adhesives, and sealants.</p></p>
