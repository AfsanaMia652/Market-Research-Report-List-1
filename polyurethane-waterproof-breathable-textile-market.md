<p><p>The Polyurethane Waterproof Breathable Textile market comprises three main types: densely woven, coated, and membrane textiles. Densely woven fabrics feature tightly interlaced fibers that prevent water penetration while allowing air circulation, making them durable and flexible. Coated textiles involve applying a polyurethane layer to a fabric, enhancing waterproofing while maintaining breathability. Membrane textiles utilize a thin layer of breathable polyurethane, allowing moisture vapor to escape while keeping water out, ideal for high-performance applications in outdoor gear and apparel.</p></p>
