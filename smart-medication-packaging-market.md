<p><p>The Smart Medication Packaging market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Modified Atmosphere Packaging** adjusts the gas composition around medications to extend shelf life and maintain potency. </p><p>2. **Active Packaging** interacts with the environment, such as absorbing moisture or releasing preservatives, to enhance product protection. </p><p>3. **Intelligent Packaging** incorporates sensors and indicators that provide real-time information about the medication's condition, ensuring safety and efficacy. Together, these packaging innovations enhance medication adherence and improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
