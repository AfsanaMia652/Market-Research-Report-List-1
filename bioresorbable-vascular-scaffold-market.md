<p><p>The bioresorbable vascular scaffold (BVS) market is primarily divided into two types: metal BVS and polymer BVS. Metal BVS, typically made from magnesium or iron alloys, offer a robust support structure while gradually dissolving in the body, facilitating vessel healing. Polymer BVS, on the other hand, are made from biodegradable polymers that provide flexibility and controlled degradation rates. Both types aim to promote natural vascular healing while minimizing long-term complications associated with permanent stents.</p></p>
