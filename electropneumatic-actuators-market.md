<p><ul><li>Single Acting Actuator</li><li>Double Acting Actuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electropneumatic actuators market consists mainly of two types: single acting and double acting actuators. Single acting actuators utilize compressed air to move the actuator in one direction, relying on a spring to return it to its original position, making them simpler and cost-effective. In contrast, double acting actuators use air pressure for both extending and retracting movements, providing greater force and control. The choice between these types depends on application requirements, including force, speed, and operational complexity.</p></p>
