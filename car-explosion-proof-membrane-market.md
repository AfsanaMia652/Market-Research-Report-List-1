<p><p>The Car Explosion-proof Membrane market includes two main types: Heat Absorption Film and Reflective Insulation Film. Heat Absorption Film is designed to absorb and dissipate heat generated during an explosion, reducing the risk of ignition and minimizing damage. In contrast, Reflective Insulation Film reflects radiant heat away from the vehicle, enhancing safety and thermal regulation. Both types serve as critical protective measures in automotive applications, contributing to vehicle resilience and occupant safety in the event of an explosion.</p></p>
