<p><ul><li>Wafer Inspection System</li><li>Mask Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor inspection systems market encompasses two primary types: wafer inspection systems and mask inspection systems. Wafer inspection systems are designed to detect defects on semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process, ensuring high yield and performance of chips. In contrast, mask inspection systems focus on the photomasks used in lithography, identifying imperfections that could affect the pattern transfer to wafers. Both systems are crucial for maintaining quality and reliability in semiconductor manufacturing, ultimately impacting the performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
