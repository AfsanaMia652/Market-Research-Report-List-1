<p><p>The auto-scrubber market includes three primary types: push-type scrubbers, ride-on scrubbers, and rear-standing scrubbers. Push-type scrubbers are manually operated and ideal for small areas, providing flexibility and ease of use. Ride-on scrubbers accommodate larger spaces, allowing operators to sit while cleaning, enhancing efficiency and reducing fatigue. Rear-standing scrubbers, designed for compact yet efficient cleaning, offer a balance between mobility and effectiveness. Each type serves distinct market needs based on facility size and operational demands.</p></p>
