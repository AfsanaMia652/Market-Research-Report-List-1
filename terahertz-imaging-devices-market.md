<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The terahertz imaging devices market encompasses two main types: passive and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging relies on detecting natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects, making it ideal for applications like security screening and material characterization. In contrast, active terahertz imaging uses external sources to generate terahertz waves, allowing for higher sensitivity and better control over imaging conditions. This type is commonly employed in biomedical applications and advanced research settings, expanding the versatility and utility of terahertz technology.</p></p>
