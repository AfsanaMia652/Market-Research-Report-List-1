<p><p>Quantitative respirator fit testing includes several key market types: PortaCount Test, CNC Test, and CNP Test. The PortaCount Test utilizes a particle counting method to assess mask fit by measuring the ratio of ambient to inside mask particles, providing real-time results. CNC Test employs a controlled negative pressure technique to evaluate the seal of respiratory protective equipment under specific conditions. CNP Test, or Controlled Negative Pressure Test, focuses on creating a negative pressure inside the mask to determine its effectiveness in providing an adequate seal against contaminants.</p></p>
