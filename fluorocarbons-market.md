Fluoroalkynes, characterized by triple bonds, find applications in advanced materials and pharmaceuticals. Perfluoroaromatic compounds, containing aromatic rings, are important in the production of high-performance materials and additives, making this market diverse and crucial for various industrial applications.</p></p>
