<p><p>The rear-facing seats market encompasses three primary types: rear-facing-only seats, convertible seats, and all-in-one seats. Rear-facing-only seats are designed exclusively for infants, featuring a lightweight design for easy transport. Convertible seats can switch from rear-facing to forward-facing as the child grows, offering longevity and versatility. All-in-one seats extend this versatility further, functioning as rear-facing, forward-facing, and booster seats, accommodating children from infancy through later years. Each type prioritizes safety and comfort for young passengers during travel.</p></p>
