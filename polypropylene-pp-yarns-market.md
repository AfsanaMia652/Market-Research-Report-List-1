<p><p>The polypropylene yarns market is primarily divided into two types: polypropylene multifilament yarn and polypropylene monofilament yarn. Polypropylene multifilament yarn consists of multiple filaments twisted together, offering strength, flexibility, and a wide range of applications, including textiles and packaging. In contrast, polypropylene monofilament yarn is a single, continuous filament that is strong and durable, commonly used in fishing lines, industrial applications, and geotextiles. Both types leverage polypropylene's advantageous properties, including lightweight, moisture resistance, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
